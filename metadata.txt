README for synthesis datasets from: Global stocks and capacity of mineral-associated soil organic carbon

Manuscript citation: Georgiou K., Jackson R. B., Vindušková O., Abramoff R. Z., Ahlström A., Feng W., Harden J. W., Pellegrini A. F. A., Polley H. W., Soong J. L., Riley W. J., Torn M. S. Global stocks and capacity of mineral-associated soil organic carbon. Nature Communications, 2022.

Corresponding data available at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5987415

Further details are provided in the manuscript and supplementary materials. 
When using data or code, please cite repositories as well as the original manuscript.

Contact: Katerina Georgiou (georgiou1@llnl.gov) with any questions.


File name: MOC_synthesis.csv 

Author = study first author
Year = study year of publication
Lat = latitude
Lon = longitude
MAT = mean annual temperature (C)
MAP = mean annual precipitation (mm/yr)
Soil.order = soil order
MineralType = dominant mineral activity (high=HM, low=LM)
Top.depth = top depth (cm)
Bottom.depth = bottom depth (cm)
Vegetation = biome category
frc_method = fraction method (density, particle size)
Clay = percent clay content (%)
Silt = percent silt content (%)
Sand = percent sand content (%)
SiltClayPercent = percent clay+silt (%)
Bulk.C = soil organic carbon concentration (gC/kg soil)
SiltClayC = mineral-associated organic carbon concentration (gC/kg soil)
POM_C = particulate organic carbon concentration (gC/kg soil)
source = designates studies included from LUCAS and ISRAD databases 


File name: synthesis_accrual.csv

study = study author and year
vegetation = vegetation type 
management = management class (managed/unmanaged)
management_detailed = management details
land_use_detailed = land use details
soil_order = soil order
time = study duration (yrs)
pro_cs = percent clay+silt content (%)
saturation_percent = percent mineralogical saturation (%)
rate_stock = carbon accrual rate (tC/ha/yr)
